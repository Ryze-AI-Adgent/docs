## We never train on your data

Your ad accounts, campaigns, and reports are **never used to train AI models**. Your data stays private and is only used to power Ryze for you.

## How processing works

Ryze uses trusted third-party AI providers to power the assistant and reporting you use through Claude.

**How it works:**

- When you ask Ryze to pull a report or make a change, your request is processed by a third-party AI provider.
- The provider uses your connected account data to generate the response or action.
- These providers do not store your data permanently.
- They do not train their models on your content.
- All processing happens under strict data agreements.

## Ad data

- We access your ad data through secure OAuth connections — we never store your ad-platform passwords.
- Your ad data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used to train AI models.
- Disconnect any integration at any time to revoke access immediately.

## Processing control

You have full control over how your data is processed:

- **Connections:** Control which ad accounts are connected.
- **Disconnect:** Remove any integration at any time.
- **Deletion:** Delete any data at any time.
- **Export:** Request a copy of all your data.
