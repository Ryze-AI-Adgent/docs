## Data ownership

You keep full ownership of your ad accounts, connections, and the data they hold. Your campaigns, reports, and account information remain exclusively yours.

We don't claim any rights to your data. It's stored privately, is **never used to train external AI models**, and is never sold or shared with third parties.

The policy is simple on purpose: your data is yours, period.

## Access control

- **Connections:** We connect to your ad platforms through OAuth — we never see or store your ad-platform passwords.
- **Scoped access:** We request only the minimum permissions each ad platform needs to do its job.
- **Disconnect anytime:** Remove any integration from the [Integrations page](https://app.get-ryze.ai/integrations) whenever you want; access is revoked immediately.
- **Isolated by default:** Your data is isolated from every other customer at the database level.
- **Deletion:** Delete your data or your entire account at any time. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed.

## Your rights

- Access all your data
- Export your data
- Delete your data at any time
- Control which accounts are connected
- Request information about how your data is used

For details on how data is stored and processed, see [Data Storage](/security/data-storage) and [Data Processing](/security/data-processing).
