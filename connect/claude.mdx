---
title: "Connect to Claude"
description: "Set up the Ryze connector in Claude and verify it works"
---

This connects Ryze to Claude so you can list accounts, pull reports, and make changes from chat. It takes about 30 seconds.

<Note>
Use **Claude Desktop** for the most reliable connection. The web version (claude.ai) works too, but it can time out on slower connections — if something stalls, switch to Desktop.
</Note>

## Step 1 — Generate your connection key

In your Ryze dashboard, open the **Ryze MCP (Claude Connector)** section at [app.get-ryze.ai/integrations](https://app.get-ryze.ai/integrations). Under **Connection keys**, name the key (e.g. "Claude Desktop"), click **Generate Key**, and copy it.

  <img src="/images/connection-key.png" alt="Generate a connection key in the Ryze dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Step 2 — Add the connector in Claude

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your account menu (bottom-left) and choose **Settings**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude-open-settings.png" alt="Open Settings from the Claude account menu" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Connectors → Customize">
    Open **Connectors**. They now live under **Customize** — click that link.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude-connectors-moved.png" alt="Connectors have moved to Customize" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a custom connector">
    In **Customize → Connectors**, click the **+** to add one.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude-add-connector.png" alt="Add a custom connector with the plus button" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the details">
    Name it **Ryze AI** and paste the URL `https://mcp.get-ryze.ai/mcp/unified/`. Expand **Advanced settings** and paste your **connection key** into the **OAuth Client ID** field, then click **Add**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/claude-connector-dialog.png" alt="Add custom connector dialog with URL and OAuth Client ID field" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3 — Verify it works

Open **Customize → Connectors** and select **Ryze AI**. A connected connector shows the Ryze URL and a long list of available tools.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/connector-connected.png" alt="Ryze AI connector connected in Claude with its tools listed" />
</Frame>

You can also test it in chat — ask Claude:

> list my ad accounts

If it returns your accounts, you're done. If it stalls, see the [FAQ](/connect/faq).
