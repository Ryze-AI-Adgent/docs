---

Connecting happens in two parts. This page covers the first: linking your ad platforms inside Ryze. The second is [connecting Ryze to Claude](/connect/claude).

## Open the Integrations page

In your Ryze dashboard, go to **Integrations** at [app.get-ryze.ai/integrations](https://app.get-ryze.ai/integrations). You'll see every supported platform with a **Connect** button.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/integrations-dashboard.png" alt="Ryze dashboard Integrations page" />
</Frame>

## Connect a platform

Click **Connect** next to the platform you want (Google Ads, Meta Ads, Google Analytics, Search Console, Shopify, and more). You'll authorize through that platform's own login — Ryze never sees or stores your platform password.

<Note>
Once your accounts are connected here, [connect Ryze to Claude](/connect/claude) so you can manage everything from chat.
</Note>

Hitting a snag with a specific platform? See the [FAQ](/connect/faq) or [contact support](/help/contact).
