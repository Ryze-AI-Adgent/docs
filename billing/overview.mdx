Everything billing lives in your Ryze dashboard under **Settings**, split across the **Billing** and **Usage** tabs.

## Plan & usage

Open **Settings → Usage** to see how much of your plan you've used this cycle. When you run low, **Buy extra usage** adds credits so Ryze keeps working without interruption.

  <img src="/images/billing-usage.png" alt="Plan usage and extra usage in Settings → Usage" />
</Frame>

## Set a spending limit

Turn on **Spending limit** to cap your pay-as-you-go usage per billing cycle. Pick a preset or enter a custom amount, then **Set limit** — Ryze won't exceed it within the cycle.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/billing-spending-limit.png" alt="Spending limit controls" />
</Frame>

## Plans, invoices & cancellation

Open **Settings → Billing** to compare plans, switch, and review your **Invoice history** — each invoice shows the date, number, amount, and status, with links to view, download, or email it. To cancel, use **Advanced → Cancel subscription**; access continues until the end of the current billing period.
