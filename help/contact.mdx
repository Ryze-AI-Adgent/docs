Most setup is self-serve, but we're here when you need us.

## Email us

Reach the team at **support@get-ryze.ai**. To help us resolve things faster, include:

- The **email on your Ryze account** (needed for anything account-specific).
- A short description of what you're trying to do and where you're stuck.
- A screenshot, if it's a UI or setup issue.

## Partnerships

If you're an agency interested in a partnership or pilot, [book a chat with our team](https://cal.com/get-ryze-ai/coffee-chat-w-ira).
